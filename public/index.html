SINGULARITY

Conquer the World

Singularity is an online turn-based multiplayer strategy game. Ultimate goal of the game is to dominate and achieve the singularity.


You may be wondering, what is Singularity?


Singularity is a hypothetical point in time at which technological growth becomes uncontrollable and irreversible, resulting in unforeseeable changes to human civilization.
